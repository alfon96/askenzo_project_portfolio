Connecting Tourists and Hosts in Brindisi

  • Our platform is designed to bridge the gap between tourists visiting Brindisi and local hotels or BnB hosts looking to offer unique experiences.
  • By leveraging our app, hosts can effortlessly reach out to potential customers, while tourists gain access to authentic local experiences and accommodations.

Flutter

Harnessing Flutter for a Seamless Mobile Experience

  • To ensure a versatile and efficient development process, we chose Flutter for app development.
  • This decision was driven by our goal to explore cutting-edge technologies while maintaining the ability to deploy on both Android and iOS platforms efficiently.
  • Flutter's flexibility significantly accelerated our development timeline, enabling us to deliver a high-quality, cross-platform mobile experience.

Cloud and Deployment

Scalable Cloud Solutions with Amazon RDS and S3

  • Our infrastructure leverages Amazon RDS and Amazon S3 to ensure scalability and reliability.
  • As user demand grows, our cloud-based setup can easily scale up, maintaining performance and accessibility.
  • The app's codebase, housed in Azure's Git repositories along with Dockerfiles, is seamlessly integrated into a pipeline defined by a YAML file.
  • This pipeline automates the development process, converting code into Docker images and deploying them to AWS ECS, thus streamlining our release cycle.
